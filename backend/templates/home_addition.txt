ARTICLE A — SCOPE OF WORK (Home Addition)
================================================================================

The following items define the agreed scope of work for this home addition
project. Any work not listed below falls outside the scope of this agreement and
will require a separate signed Change Order.

  ✓  1 Site Visit / Existing Conditions Assessment
  ✓  Existing Floor Plan (as-built survey)
  ✓  Site Plan (property boundaries, setbacks, existing structures)
  ✓  Proposed Addition Floor Plan
  ✓  Proposed Exterior Elevations (all affected sides)
  ✓  Roof Plan (existing + proposed)
  ✓  Building Sections (minimum 2 cross-sections)
  ✓  Window & Door Schedule
  ✓  Title 24 Energy Compliance Report
  ✓  Structural Coordination Notes (structural engineer required separately)
  ✓  Building Permit Drawing Set (permit-ready)
  ✓  City Plan Check Coordination (up to 3 rounds of corrections)
  ✓  Updates based on within-scope corrections from the city

Note: Any work not listed above falls outside the scope of this agreement and
will require a separate signed Change Order. Structural engineering drawings are
NOT included and must be contracted separately with a licensed engineer.
